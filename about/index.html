About SoftEdit Tools

SoftEdit Tools is a free collection of simple online utilities built for everyday tasks. Every tool on this site runs entirely in your browser — no account, no sign-up, no data sent to a server. You open it, you use it, you move on.

Why We Built This

Most online tools are cluttered with ads, pop-ups, and dark patterns that push you to sign up for something. We wanted the opposite: tools that do exactly one thing, do it well, and get out of the way. That philosophy — simple tools, softly made — shapes every decision on this site, from the interface design to the absence of a mandatory login.

The name "SoftEdit" reflects that spirit. Soft, because nothing here is aggressive or pushy. Edit, because most of our tools help you work with text — counting, converting, generating, checking.

We also publish a blog with short, practical articles on topics related to our tools — character limits for social platforms, essay length guides, unit conversion explanations, and more.

How the Tools Work

Every tool on this site processes your input locally in your browser using JavaScript. Nothing you type is sent to any server. This means the tools work even without an internet connection once the page has loaded, and it means your text stays private by design — not just by policy.
