Frequently asked questions

How do I convert units online?

Select the category (e.g. Length), pick your source unit in the From dropdown and the target unit in the To dropdown, then type your value into the Amount field. The converted result appears in real time — no need to press a button.

How many centimeters are in an inch?

There are exactly 2.54 centimeters in one inch. This is a defined constant — 1 inch = 2.54 cm by international agreement since 1959.

How do I convert Celsius to Fahrenheit?

Multiply the Celsius value by 9/5, then add 32. In formula form: °F = (°C × 1.8) + 32. For example, 100 °C = (100 × 1.8) + 32 = 212 °F. To go the other way: °C = (°F − 32) ÷ 1.8.

How many pounds are in a kilogram?

One kilogram equals approximately 2.20462 pounds. Equivalently, one pound equals about 0.453592 kilograms.

How many MB are in a GB?

In computing, 1 gigabyte (GB) equals 1,024 megabytes (MB) using binary prefixes (as reported by most operating systems). Under the SI decimal definition, 1 GB = 1,000 MB. This converter uses the binary (1024) definition common to OS file sizes.
