How to use this currency converter

Enter any amount in the field above, then select your source currency from the "From" dropdown and your target currency from the "To" dropdown. The converted result updates instantly as you type — no button click needed.

Use the ⇄ swap button between the dropdowns to instantly reverse the conversion direction. For the most common pairs, click any button in the "Popular conversions" row to auto-fill both dropdowns in one tap.

Rates are fetched live from ExchangeRate-API each time you load the page and cached for the duration of your session. The "Rates updated" timestamp below the result shows exactly when the data was last refreshed.

Frequently asked questions

How accurate are the exchange rates?

Rates are sourced from ExchangeRate-API, which aggregates data from central banks and financial data providers. The values closely track mid-market rates and are suitable for informational use. For actual financial transactions, check with your bank or broker — they apply their own spreads and fees on top of the mid-market rate.

How often are exchange rates updated?

On the free tier, ExchangeRate-API refreshes rates once every 24 hours. Rates are fetched fresh each time you load this page. The "Rates updated" note below the result shows the exact date the data was last published.
