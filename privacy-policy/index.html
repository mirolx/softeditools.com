Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 7, 2026

SoftEdit Tools ("we," "our," or "us") operates the website softeditools.com. This privacy policy explains what information is collected when you visit our site, how it is used, and what choices you have. We keep it straightforward because our tools are simple by design.

Information We Collect

SoftEdit Tools does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to use our tools. All tools run entirely in your browser — text you type into the word counter, character counter, or any other tool is never sent to our servers.

However, like most websites, we collect certain information automatically when you visit:

  • Log data: Your browser type, operating system, referring URL, pages visited, and the date and time of your visit. This information is collected by our hosting provider in standard server logs.
  • Cookies: Small files stored in your browser, primarily placed by third-party services described below. We do not set our own tracking cookies.

Google AdSense and Advertising

We display advertisements served by Google AdSense. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our site and other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to SoftEdit Tools and other sites on the internet.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings. Alternatively, you can opt out of a third-party vendor's use of cookies for personalized advertising by visiting aboutads.info.

How We Use Information

The information collected automatically is used solely to:

  • Understand how visitors use the site so we can improve it
  • Diagnose technical problems
  • Prevent abuse

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to third parties except as described in this policy (Google AdSense) or as required by law.

Third-Party Links

Some of our blog posts and tool pages may contain links to external websites. We have no control over the privacy practices of those sites and are not responsible for their content or policies. We recommend reviewing the privacy policy of any third-party site you visit.

Children's Privacy

SoftEdit Tools is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the site after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
