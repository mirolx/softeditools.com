Frequently asked questions

What is Lorem Ipsum?

Lorem Ipsum is placeholder text that has been used in the publishing and typesetting industry since the 1500s. It is derived from a work of classical Latin literature by Cicero, scrambled so it is no longer meaningful — making it perfect for filling visual space without distracting readers with real content.

Why do designers use Lorem Ipsum?

Designers use Lorem Ipsum — also called dummy text or placeholder text — because it lets you focus on layout, typography, and visual hierarchy rather than the words themselves. When real copy is used in early mockups, viewers tend to read and critique the content instead of evaluating the design.

Is Lorem Ipsum real Latin?

Almost, but not quite. Lorem Ipsum is based on de Finibus Bonorum et Malorum (On the Ends of Good and Evil) written by Cicero in 45 BC, but the words have been altered, shuffled, and some invented so the result does not form grammatically correct Latin. It simply looks and flows like real Latin text.

How many words is a Lorem Ipsum paragraph?

A typical Lorem Ipsum paragraph contains between 50 and 100 words, spread across four to eight sentences. This free lorem ipsum generator produces paragraphs of similar length so they feel natural in any design layout.

Can I use Lorem Ipsum for commercial projects?

Yes. Lorem Ipsum text is in the public domain and can be used freely in any project — commercial or otherwise. Just remember to replace it with real content before publishing. Leaving placeholder dummy text in a live product can look unprofessional.

What is dummy text used for?

Dummy text (also called filler text or placeholder text) is used wherever real content is not yet available — in web design mockups, print layouts, app prototypes, email templates, and more. It helps communicate the intended structure and feel of a design before the actual copy is written.
